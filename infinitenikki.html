what is infinite nikki?

infinite nikki is a game released only a few days ago (dec. 4th) ...it is an open world dress up game. in this game, you explore the world, finding clothing blueprints to craft clothes and partake in styling battles / dress up. the main character is nikki, and her best friend momo.

in this game, you explore the world of miraland after mysteriously ending up in this world. you can be many different things-- grooming animals, catching bugs, floating above the sky, fishing... there is a lot to offer. it is kind of like "play now, gacha later" type of game. there's no rush to roll-- just to enjoy your exploration first, and figuring out the story.

i haven't played this game very much so far, but i really like the dress up part.. the point of this page is just to show off my outfits, photography, and share how pretty the game is °˖✧◝(⁰▿⁰)◜✧˖° i love the dress up part, and how easy and sweet it feels, like a dream. and hope the more that i can play this game i can share more about it!
