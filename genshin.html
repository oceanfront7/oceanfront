welcome to teyvat

hello, and welcome to my genshin page! i've been playing genshin since january 2021. i am currently AR level 60.

my history of genshin...

i first began playing genshin after a friend introduced me to it. i believe i was in 7th or 8th grade... which is insane time flies so quickly. i joined in version 1.2, when ganyu & albedo came out. instantly, i became OBSESSED! genshin is the game i have played the most. i assumedly have around 23,000 hours in game, and i've logged in around 1,060 days-- that is almost 2 years!

anyway, 2021 was the year i played the most. i geniunely believe i played every single day that year. in 2022, i also played an incredible ammount. by around early april 2021, i was AR 41 already! but the start of 2023 was when i began to stop playing, which was also when i was in my freshman year of high school (somewhere between 3.2-3.4). after this, i rarely logged in. i am still not sure why i became so disinterested: it couldve been because at the time sumeru was not very popular, and then after that came the worst nation of natlan, etc. or also, because i had had the major life change of high school, changing schedules, more work, etc. i think even just a short absence snowballed, as coming back would instantly make me feel overwhelmed. i pretty much missed all limited-time content. here are some photos from my early days...

anyway, after 2023, i did miss a lot. i got through sumeru and fontaine quests, but that was about it. i only idly kept up, and got all of my information from seeing new content on instagram or something of the sort. it was only now, in 2026, that all the sudden i had some sort of calling to play again. i legitimately have no idea what caused this, but now, i can't stop playing again.

honestly, i believe this is because im a senior now and its right around the time ive finished applying to college and honestly worrying about most things. i feel much more refereshed than i have in a long time, and its allowing me to get back into a lot of things i lost while in high school.

i reached AR 59 on march 11 2023. i didn't make it to AR 60 until february 2026... lol

anyway, i made this section of my website to be an archive of my past photos, thoughts, and everything else with this game. this game has seen every part of me. i have cried, laughed, smiled, gotten angry at, and loved this game. it really is a significant part of my life, even when i have a significant hiatus. genshin is so full of things to do, of lore, interesting characters, etc... it is my 'life game'. so, i hope you enjoy this page!
