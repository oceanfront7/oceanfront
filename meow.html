About

ari is a vampire from the underworld whom the whole world seems to revolve around. despite being polar opposites, she’s the partner of lux, and best friends with cake & casy. growing up in the underworld, she processes life rather differently than others: she can be cunning, arrogant, reckless, and selfish, but extremely protective and caring towards her friends. she is a realist and pessimist, full of energy and bundled up emotions. she can be rather hotheaded and won’t hesitate to share how she feels. regardless, with everything she has been through, she has become a true friend and companion.

background

ari was born in the underworld, the land below albie that was home to nocturnal creatures and more nefarious people than usual. her father controlled the souls of the deceased, and her mother was mainly.. just there. when she was only a few years old, her mother became fatally ill and passed. however; she wasn't very bothered by it-- as her father controlled the spirits, he would also have a watch over what would happen to her. this would translate into ari's life later on-- not really giving a damn about life and death, giving her an unervingly bold outlook on life. ari spent her childhood playing with other underworld children, reading, and playing made-up games. she became close friends with a boy named angus, and they formed a close bond.

however, as time would pass, she grew tired of looking at the same things everyday, doing the same things, and realized through word of mouth that there was really much more out in the world than in a dark, infinite underground cavern. she decided that it was time for her to leave-- and she began a long journey of crossing into the overworld. the gate was made of thousands of steps, which she spent months climbing. she had started by flying up the stairs, but a stray root of a branch caught her in the side. she lost the ability to fly, and would later saw her wings off at the root. she spent a birthday on the journey with just herself and some stuffed animals she bagged up. when she did finally reach the top of the stairs, she had to start acclimating to the light a few hundred steps before. the sun was so bright that it temporarily blinded her, and damaged her skin. she had no idea what season it was, but a relative idea of what the date was. she was overwhelmed yet fascinated at the ammount of life, nature, and beauty the world had-- so shocked that she spent days wandering in the same spot.

at one point, she discovered a pack of wolves. naturally, the chased after her when she tried to approach them, and she ran away crying. as she ran, she fell down into a crater-like field of lavender. definitely someone's field, but reguardless, even though her life was on the line, she calmed down quickly. this field was new and beautiful to her. but if those weren't enough, she began to see a floating object coming down from the sky.. slowly... a girl, riding a star-shaped object, floating down.. a girl, around the same age as ari, floated down to her level. her body was a conglomerate of stars, dust, and universal objects. seriously, what was going on in this upper world.. tf? it was cake-- who would become her new traveling partner and best friend.they both were there to explore with no real plans-- this strange, alien girl.. anyway. they spent quite some time traveling through towns, cities, countrysides. until one day, they had been taking a stroll in a forest nearby to the village they were staying in. eating bread and chatting, they stumbled upon a mysterious pond in the forest. there was something in the pond-- no, it was someone; it was .. casy. only then, he was a merman. his fear of humans was still with him, and he lashed out when they tried approaching him. it took hours for them to convince him they weren't hunting him down, and were there to be friends. they spent the next few days with him as he changed in an out of mermaid form. ari thought casy to be a little.. strange, considering his shy personality, and wondered why he was so worked up all the time. ari and cake actually combined enough money to buy a small tub, put some wheels on it, so if he ever changed while on the road, there wouldn't be a problem.

as a year passes, ari notices cake and casy's relationship develop. around this time, she feels rather lonley. while they're still close friends, she wants to seek about more things in life. she longs for more friends, and more experience. as she does, however, her group comes across another person. a frail, but determined person. she meets lux, on a morning where he's training archery. this morning she was out walking by herself when she met him. at first he raised his arrow towards her, seeing her as a threat. but she quickly raised her hands up. they got to talking, and although he wasn't keen on traveling with her just yet, he did accept the offer to stay the night with them. by the 3rd day, he realized he could get along with them well-- they had both found what they had been looking for, in different ways.

now, they all live in a small cottage in a town. it's near a forest and a beach, so there's many places to go and things to see, but after awhile.. not much. ari longs for more exploring, but for now she needs to stay where things matter most. since she left home, she's never vacationed back, and hasn't spoken to anyone she used to know. reguardless, she's happy either way.

relationships

lux [ partner ]

lux is ari's current partner. she loves him incredibly so, probably more than he does. while it is her first time being in a relationship with someone, she's often a little unsure where the boundries are, or when they exist. however, she tries her best to make lux happy, always giving him the best of everything and making sure he's alright. she wants to be the person he can come to when he's in fear of the future or past. they have a healthy, happy, strange teenage relationship.

cake [ best friend ]

cake is the first person ari met above the world and her best friend. they both have a level of airheadedness that levels out with one another, making them easy to get along with and great at sharing secrets with eachother. cake encourages ari to always go shopping, and ari encourages her to .. do less than morally accurate things. they were closest when they were little kids.

casy [ best friend ]

ari and casy have completely oppisite personalities. for awhile, he was hesitant around her, only really sticking around cake's side. but he realized that ari was, while a bit eccentric, still a person, and began to open up to her. they have had a lot of conversations, mostly deeper ones, and ari likes to get him out of his head and look at the bigger picture. albeit she likes to pick on him often.

miki [ close friend ]

miki and ari don't have a set place or time when they met. their irregular occuring meetings are sparatic and unset. they are the type of friends who won't talk for months, but can still act like no time at all has passed when they speak to eachother again. they have similar personalities, but miki keeps her anger hidden inside of her. they gossip frequently (and have a lot of AU's around eachother bc they're my two most used characters lolll)

tsundere [ friend ]

tsundere is very good friends with ari. while ari relays a lot of her personal info to him, he mostly stays silent. they are around eachother so often that at times people will mistake them for siblings or partners. in reality, they just watch modern housewives or something.....

angus [ old friend ]

angus is ari's closest childhood friend. she hasn't spoken to him since she left, but their memories are strong. she would confide in him with typical childhood "secrets" and the sorts, but she wasn't aware of his crush on her. she is unaware of his burning feelings and his pain from her leaving him, but i don't really think she would... care... ngl..

rory [ acquaintance ]

ari has really only talked to rory when with tsundere. she admires rory because of her boldness, but rory finds ari a little stupid, thinking they are so alike...

sky [ enemy ]

ari is sky's unknown enemy. while she's unaware wether he's alive or not, he is one she's actively plotting against for hurting lux and being one of the main causes of his problems. she's not aware that it wasn't really sky's doing, but she levels her hatred out with the anger against the other angels who were hurt for his expense. she.. really doesn't like him, even though she only knows him through word
